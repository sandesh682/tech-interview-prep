## 🔥 Top 10 Most Asked Node.js Interview Questions

1. Explain the Event Loop and its phases
2. What is the difference between `process.nextTick` and `setImmediate`?
3. How does Node.js handle concurrency if it's single-threaded?
4. What are streams and why use them over `readFileSync`?
5. How does Express middleware work? What is `next()`?
6. How do you prevent memory leaks in Node.js?
7. Explain JWT — structure, signing, verification
8. What is connection pooling and why is it important?
9. How do you scale a Node.js application?
10. CommonJS vs ES Modules — key differences
