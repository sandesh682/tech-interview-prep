
# 📌 LinkedIn Daily Job Hunt System

## 🔍 1. Search Strategy
- Apply filters: Location, Experience, Date (Past 24h/Week), Job Type
- Switch to "Most Recent"
- Gradually remove filters if results are low

## 📊 2. Evaluate Job
- Check match with JD (60–70%+)
- Check company size (50–1000 preferred)
- Verify tech stack (MERN), role fit, notice period

## 🚀 3. Apply Smartly
- Prefer Easy Apply / Direct Apply
- Avoid random mass applying
- Target 10–20 quality applications daily

## 💬 4. Message Recruiters
- High match → InMail
- Medium match → Connect + Note

### Connect Note:
Hi [Name], I came across a MERN Stack role at your company. I have 6+ years of experience in React, Node.js, and scalable APIs. I’ve applied and would love to connect. Thanks!

### InMail:
Hi [Name],  
I noticed the MERN Stack role at [Company]. I have 6+ years experience in React, Node.js, and backend systems. I’ve applied and would appreciate the opportunity to discuss. Thanks!

## 🔁 5. Daily Targets
- Apply: 10–20 jobs
- Message: 5–10 recruiters
- Follow-up after 2–3 days

## 📢 6. LinkedIn Growth
- Post 1–2 times per week (learning, projects, insights)
- Engage with others (like/comment daily)

## 📚 7. Skill Improvement
- Keep learning new skills (DSA, System Design, MERN depth)
- Regularly update LinkedIn skills & resume
- Add new projects/work samples

## ⚡ Pro Tips
- Apply within 24 hours of posting
- Focus on startups / mid-size companies
- Track applications (Company | Role | Date | Status)
- Consistency = results (7–10 days to see traction)
